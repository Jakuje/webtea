What is webTea?

WebTea is simple stopwatch helping you to make a tea, toasts or egg.

How to use webTea?

On the main screen you can see a two widgets. First widget contains a list of basic types of Tea and it's recomanded steeping times. You can choose from the list here or set your own time in the next widget. After that, you simply click the "Start" button and there apears a countdown.

Can I add my own times?

It's in to-do list. Be patient.

Could I use my own time?

Ofcourse! You can simply write a number (1 to 15) on keyboard, or tap time input to choose your own time.
